About Small But Perfectly Formed

Small but Perfectly Formed is a cross Europe collaborative project that will accelerate fashion SMEâ€™s to transition to work within circular and sustainable business models. Small but Perfectly Formed recognises that small and medium size fashion enterprises are working within a wider system and need more than acceleration; they need to be integrated into a system that truly puts people and planet before profit. Therefore, Small but Perfectly Formed aims to drive systemic change in the fashion business support landscape through 'accelerating the accelerators', mapping the policy landscape in which SMEs operate and creating a transnational network of SMEs and organisations who support them.
